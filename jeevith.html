Wikipedia

Wikipedia is a free online encyclopedia with articles written by volunteers who write without pay, and its articles can be edited by anyone with internet access. Wikipedia is hosted by the Wikimedia Foundation, a non-profit organization.

History

Wikipedia was launched on January 15, 2001, by Jimmy Wales and Larry Sanger. It is one of the largest reference websites on the internet, with millions of articles in various languages.

Usage

Wikipedia is widely used as a reference source for general information, and its content is available freely under a Creative Commons license. Users can contribute by editing articles or creating new ones.
